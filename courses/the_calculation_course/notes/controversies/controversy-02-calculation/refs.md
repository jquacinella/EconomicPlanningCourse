# References — Controversy 2 — Socialist Calculation

_Papers, books, side topics to come back to._

## Neurath and the *Naturalrechnung* tradition

- Otto Neurath, "Total Socialization" (1920) and "Economic Plan and Calculation in Kind" (1925), in *Otto Neurath: Economic Writings 1904–1945*, ed. Thomas Uebel & Robert Cohen (Kluwer 2004). Canonical English-language collection.
- Otto Neurath, *Durch die Kriegswirtschaft zur Naturalwirtschaft* (1919). German original; the position Mises 1920 is replying to.
- Thomas Uebel, "Calculation in Kind and Marketless Socialism: On Otto Neurath's Utopian Economics" (*European Journal of the History of Economic Thought*, 2005).
- Thomas Uebel, *Otto Neurath: A Bio-Bibliography* (Vienna Circle Institute Yearbook, multiple volumes) — for biographical and intellectual context.
- John O'Neill, "In Partial Praise of a Positivist: The Work of Otto Neurath" (*Radical Philosophy* 74, 1995). Free online; the best short entry point.
- John O'Neill, *The Market: Ethics, Knowledge, and Politics* (Routledge 1998), Chapter 8.

## Primary calculation-debate texts

- Ludwig von Mises, "Economic Calculation in the Socialist Commonwealth" (1920). Free PDF at Mises Institute.
- Friedrich Hayek, "The Use of Knowledge in Society" (*American Economic Review* 1945).
- Oskar Lange, "On the Economic Theory of Socialism" (*Review of Economic Studies* 4, 1936).
- Don Lavoie, *Rivalry and Central Planning: The Socialist Calculation Debate Reconsidered* (Cambridge 1985).

## Cockshott / Cottrell / Dapprich

- Paul Cockshott & Allin Cottrell, "Information and Economics: A Critique of Hayek" (1997). Free PDF at Glasgow.
- Allin Cottrell & Paul Cockshott, "Calculation, Complexity and Planning" (*Review of Political Economy*, 1993).
- Cockshott & Cottrell, *Towards a New Socialism* (1993). Free PDF.
- Jan-Daniel Dapprich & David Greenwood, "Cybersocialism and the Future of the Socialist Calculation Debate" (*Studies in Political Economy*, 2024).

## Contemporary Austrian counter-position

- Peter Boettke & Rosolino Candela, "On the Feasibility of Technosocialism" (*Journal of Economic Behavior & Organization* 205, 2023).
