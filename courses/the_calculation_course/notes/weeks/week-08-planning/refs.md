## Naturalrechnung as multi-resource LP

- John O'Neill, "In Partial Praise of a Positivist: The Work of Otto Neurath" (*Radical Philosophy* 74, 1995). Best short entry point for the connection between *Naturalrechnung* and modern multi-resource LP.
- Joan Martinez-Alier, *Ecological Economics: Energy, Environment and Society* (Blackwell 1987), Chapter 11 ("Otto Neurath and the Calculation Debates").
- Otto Neurath, *Economic Writings 1904–1945*, ed. Uebel & Cohen (Kluwer 2004), for primary sources.
- See also Appendix D (Calculation in Kind) for the multi-criteria optimization formalism.
